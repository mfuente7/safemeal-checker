How To Use The App

To search by food name, type something like cereal, chips, or chocolate into the first search box and press Search Food.

To search by barcode, enter a barcode number into the second search box. The app will try to find the exact product from Open Food Facts.

If you want to save a product, click Save Favorite on the food card. The product will be saved in the Supabase database.

Some products may not show every detail because Open Food Facts depends on the product information that has already been added by users.
