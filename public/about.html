About This Project

SafeMeal Checker was made to help people understand food labels faster. A lot of people do not have time to read every ingredient while shopping, especially when the label is long or confusing.

The app uses the Open Food Facts API to search real food products. It can show the product name, brand, image, ingredients, allergens, and nutrition grade when that information is available.

The project also uses Supabase to save favorite products. This makes the app more useful because users can come back and see products they wanted to remember.
